# Emaniyat-privacy

Privacy Policy for Emaniyat
Last updated: 02/22/2026

Emaniyat is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information when you create an account or update your profile:
Phone number.
Password.
First name.
Last name.
City.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
Create and manage your account.
Allow you to log in securely.
Personalize your user experience.
Provide customer support.
Improve and maintain the App's functionality.
We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties.
3. Third-Party Services
We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications.
For notifications, FCM collects:
A unique device token (used only to send notifications to your device).
FCM does not access your personal profile information. It only manages push notification delivery.
4. Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely on our servers.
We use standard technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.
Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text.
5. Account & Data Deletion
You can delete your account at any time through the Delete Account option located in the App's Settings.
When you delete your account:
All personal data associated with your account (phone number, name, gender, date of birth, city) is permanently deleted from our system.
The deletion is irreversible.
Your data is removed immediately.
